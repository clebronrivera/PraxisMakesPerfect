# Design Context — Praxis Makes Perfect

## Target Audience
Graduate students preparing for the Praxis 5403 (School Psychology) licensure exam. Mostly early-career professionals (22–30), studying on their own time, often stressed and overwhelmed. Not developers — regular people on laptops/Macs. They come to the product having already studied for weeks and still feeling lost about what to actually focus on.

## Core Use Case
Adaptive diagnostic assessment that pinpoints exactly which of the 4 exam domains (Professional Practice, Assessment, Intervention & Mental Health, Foundations) the user is weak in — so they stop wasting time reviewing material they already know and go straight to what will cost them points.

## Jobs to Be Done
1. "Tell me what I actually need to study" (primary)
2. "Give me targeted practice on my weak areas"
3. "Help me understand concepts I keep getting wrong" (AI Tutor)
4. "Show me a plan to get ready before my test date" (Study Plan)

## Brand Personality
- **Confident, not aggressive** — like a smart study coach who's seen this before
- **Efficient, not cold** — respects that users are busy and stressed
- **Precise, not clinical** — data-driven but human
- **Empowering, not overwhelming** — the point is to reduce anxiety, not add to it

## Tone
Direct. Clear. Purposeful. No fluff. Every word earns its place.
Not corporate. Not playful. Not gamified. Test-prep adjacent — but smarter than the incumbents (Kaplan, Princeton Review).

## What Makes This Memorable
The diagnostic result reveal — users go in not knowing what to study, and come out with a precise map of their gaps. That moment of "oh, THAT'S why I keep failing" is the core emotional beat.

## Technical Constraints
- Standalone HTML mockups (Tailwind CDN, no build step)
- Must work without authentication
- Bottom nav bar for screen-to-screen navigation in mockups
- No framework — vanilla HTML/JS only for mockups

## Key Screens
1. Hero / landing page
2. Pre-assessment (Adaptive Diagnostic vs Spicy Mode)
3. Diagnostic in progress
4. Loading/reveal animation
5. Dashboard home
6. Practice tab (with spaced review badges)
7. AI Tutor (with Digital/PDF activity modal)

## Design Anti-Goals
- No purple-to-blue gradients everywhere
- No glassmorphism cards
- No "AI product" look (cyan accents, neon glows, dark mode defaults)
- No stock-photo test-prep look (pencils, graduation caps, etc.)
- No gamification (badges, XP bars, confetti)
