A school psychologist is developing a crisis response plan. Which of the following should be the first priority?

A Ensuring student and staff physical safety ✓
B Documenting the incident for the school board ✗
C Conducting individual counseling sessions
D Notifying parents of all students

Correct! Physical safety is always the top priority in crisis response.

PREPaRE model (Brock et al.) establishes physical safety as the foundational step before any psychological intervention.
