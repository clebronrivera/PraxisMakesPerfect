Confidence

A parent requests an initial evaluation for their 7-year-old child, who they suspect has a learning disability. The school conducts the evaluation within 60 days, finds the student does not qualify for special education services, and sends the family a copy of the report. According to IDEA, which of the following must also be provided to the parents?

A
A written plan describing how the school will review the student's progress in general education.
B
Prior written notice explaining the refusal to provide services, along with a copy of the procedural safeguards.
C
A list of outside agencies that may provide private evaluation services at the family's expense.
D
A 504 plan draft outlining accommodations the student may receive in the general classroom.
Correct · After submit

IDEA requires prior written notice any time a school proposes or refuses to initiate or change identification, evaluation, educational placement, or FAPE. A copy of the procedural safeguards notice must be given at least once a year and always in connection with this kind of decision.

Why B A & D are non-sequiturs post-refusal · C mischaracterizes IDEA

Skill context

IDEA & FAPE — the federal framework guaranteeing a free, appropriate public education to students with disabilities. Tested constructs: evaluation procedures, LRE, IEP components, procedural safeguards.

Decision rule: when a school refuses eligibility, parents must receive PWN + procedural safeguards.
