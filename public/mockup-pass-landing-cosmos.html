The science

Six principles
borrowed from cognitive science.

Most prep apps gamify. PASS engineers — every interaction is grounded in decades of replicated findings on how durable knowledge forms.

01

Retrieval Practice

Pulling answers out, not pushing them in. We replace re-reading with active recall — the single biggest lever in cognitive science.

02

Spaced Repetition

Items resurface right before you forget them. Each successful retrieval bumps the interval; each miss tightens it.

03

Interleaving

Skills are mixed across domains, not blocked. Harder in the moment, far more durable on test day.

04

Desirable Difficulty

Friction that helps. We tune item difficulty to your edge — hard enough to learn, easy enough to keep going.

05

Confidence Calibration

Every answer asks how sure you are. Miscalibration is the silent test killer; we surface it.

06

Mastery Modeling

A Bayesian skill model tracks the blueprint beneath the exam. We don't move on until the math says you're ready.
