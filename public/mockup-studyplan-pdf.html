Use these checkpoints to decide whether to advance, repeat, or escalate. The goal isn't completing sessions — it's confirming that corrections are holding and near-mastery skills are crossing the threshold.

3 consecutive correct answers in practice (not just more attempts)
No repeat of the flagged wrong distractor in the last 5 attempts
Accuracy rises above 65% with "Sure" confidence on at least 2 correct answers
If not clearing: Re-read the misconception note for that skill, then do the Learning Path module section before any more practice questions.
🟡 Near-mastery skill — pushed to Demonstrating when:
Accuracy crosses 80% on the dashboard (confidence-weighted)
Minimum 8 total attempts — don't call it mastered on 5 easy questions
Trend shows Improving or Stable over last 6 attempts
If stalling at 70–79%: Identify which domain of the skill is weak using the answer history. Likely a single conceptual sub-area, not the whole skill.
