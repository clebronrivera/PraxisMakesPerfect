SKILL DOMAIN SUMMARY
================================================================================

DBD-08 focuses on:
  • Oral Reading Fluency (ORF) as words-correct-per-minute measure
  • Maze comprehension fluency (cloze task under time)
  • Early literacy CBMs (NWF, LNF, PSF) and their bounds
  • Slope calculation, aimline projection, trend-vs-noise discrimination
  • Standard Error of Measurement (SEM) confidence interpretation
  • CBM benchmarks, universal screening, dual-discrepancy frameworks
  • RtI standard-protocol advantages and limitations
  • Progress-monitoring frequency by intensity tier
  • Math CBM scoring (digits-correct-per-minute)
  • CBM vs. CBA conceptual distinction

Most Common Reasoning Failures:
  1. Conflating constructs (e.g., ORF as comprehension proxy)
  2. Skipping slope-to-aimline math before interpreting adequacy
  3. Overstating scope of single measures
  4. Misattributing responsibility (e.g., motivation vs. instruction)
  5. Treating benchmarks as absolutes vs. local reference points

Most Effective Teaching Moves:
  • Explicit slope arithmetic with projection to goal
  • Concept boundary drawing (individual progress vs. school audit)
  • Emphasis on "frequent, short, local" as CBM's defining strength
  • Distractor-specific remediations (not generic advice)
